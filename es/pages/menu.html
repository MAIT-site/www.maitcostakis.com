My great-grandfather, Ryzhik lived with George Costakis and was very close to him. Costakis taught him all the tricks of art collecting, and these teachings have been passed down through generations in our family. That's why someone from our family always keeps an eye on the museum.


Our museum holds many remarkable stories and captivating legends, both about George Costakis and the paintings.
